Rochelle's Cupcake Recipe

Pretty, delicious pink cupcakes that brighten any day!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2-3 drops of pink food coloring

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake tin with 12 liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the sugar and butter together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and beat until smooth.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture, alternating with the milk, mixing until well combined.
  6. Add 2-3 drops of pink food coloring and mix until the batter turns a soft pink.
  7. Spoon the batter into the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  8. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the cupcakes cool before frosting with your favorite icing!
